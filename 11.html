The "10 + 1" Rule 📐

2/6

Splitting 11

Multiplying by 11 is the same as:

  • 1. Multiplying your number by 10.
  • 2. Multiplying your number by 1.
  • 3. Adding those two results together!

It works because 11 is just 10 + 1!

Let's Try with 43!

Step 1: 43 × 10 = 430
Step 2: 43 × 1 = 43
➔ 430 + 43 = 473

Why We Add the Middle! 🧐

3/6

Watch the Columns!

When we add 430 and 43 together, see how the digits align:

- The 4 is in the Hundreds column.
- The 3 is in the Ones column.
- In the middle (Tens column), 4 and 3 overlap!

That is why we just add the two digits in the middle: 4 + 3 = 7.
